Home of the Philadelphia Eagles

Quick Facts

The Eagles are a professional American footaball team based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
The team compete in the National Footnball League (NFL) as part of the NFC East division.
The team repeatedly ranks as one of the best in the league in attendance and has sold out
every game since 1999. In a poll of numerous NFL Players by Sports Illustrated, Eagles fans
were voted to be the most intmidating.

Our True Home

Lincoln Financial Field, our "true home", is the home stadium of the Eagles. It is located
in South Philadelphia on Pattison Avenue alonside I-95 as part of the Sports Complex. The
stadium is noted for hosting numerous events, including the 2005 NFC Championship Game, the
2015 CONCACAF Gold Cup Final, and the 2018 NFL Kickoff Game.
